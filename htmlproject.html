Social Media Bad

Social media use among teens has risen over the past decade or so. In 2008 only 43% of teens used social media. Today, 94% of teens use social media and 26% use it for over 8 hours per day.

    • As social media use has increased, so have suicide related hospital admissions among teens. They have almost tripled from 2008 to 2015, increasing from 0.67% to 1.79%.
